About us

Plain answers for a confusing purchase

Buying a car involves more moving parts than almost any other purchase most people make regularly — pricing, financing, trade-ins, warranties, and a sales process that's designed to move fast. Car Buying Handbook exists to slow that down.

We publish straightforward guides on negotiating, financing, and inspecting a car, plus free calculators so you can check the numbers yourself instead of taking someone else's word for them. Nothing here requires an account or an email address — the tools are free to use as often as you like.

We're not a dealership, lender, or car marketplace. We don't sell cars, and we don't take referral fees from any dealer we mention. Our guides are informational, not personalized financial or legal advice — always confirm details relevant to your situation with your lender or a qualified professional.
